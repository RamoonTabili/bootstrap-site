We call ourselves Climate Reverse

We try to acomplish what out name means; putting the cliimate in reverse to what it was before.

We are a small non-profit organization with a small team of 10 members that started Climate Reverse during the Earth Day in the April of 2016. We do a variety of activities to try to raise both awareness and funds to battle against the changing weather. We could always use some volunteer help and we are always open to new ideas and methods on decreasing our carbon footprint from our home we call Earth.

Mission? Save Earth

We belive that tackling climate change isn’t just the right thing to do. It’s also a good thing to do – good for our economy, good for public health, and good for our communities. We want to act as a voice for the environment, nationally and internationally, working with others to inspire the renewal of our communities and the earth, through research, education and advocacy.

The challenge

Current greenhouse gas emission trends put the world on course for a 3.7-4.8°C temperature increase by 2100, which would cause catastrophic effects. Even the commitments made under the new Paris Agreement fall short of the cuts required to limit warming to a relatively safer 2°C. Even if all emissions are stopped immediately, effects will continue for centuries due to the cumulative impact of emissions already in the atmosphere. Meanwhile, nearly 800 million people globally are currently considered especially vulnerable to the effects of climate change.
